Privacy Policy

How we handle information.

This policy explains what Technostics Group collects through the website and contact form, why it is collected, and how it is used.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, email address, company name, service choice, and the message you submit through the contact form.

How we use it

We use submitted details only to respond to inquiries, prepare proposals, and manage service discussions.

How we store it

Messages may be stored in Formspree and in our email inbox for follow-up. We do not sell personal information.

Third parties

The site uses Formspree to receive form submissions. Their handling of data is subject to their own policies and controls.

Your choices

You can request that we delete information you have submitted, except where we need to retain it for legal, accounting, or security reasons.

If you have questions about this policy, use the contact page and include "privacy" in the subject or message.
